Milan set
Simplicity at its core, these cabinets do exactly what they need to. The Milan Set is the most affordable kitchen cabinetry available and is perfect for anyone on a restricted budget, or those who simply require a basic unit.
Milan set is also well suited for rooms outside of the kitchen; such as laundries, basements and even sleepouts. All of our cabinets are made in Italy and come with step by step instructions, making assembly simple - even for those who aren't DIY minded.
  • Three Year Warranty
  • Moisture Resistant Particle Board Door
  • Italian Hardware
  • Individual Toekick & Handle Included
  • White Satin Melamine Finish

  • Five Year Warranty
